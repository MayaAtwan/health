Document title: High-Protein Ingredients Guide for This Recipe Collection

Purpose:
This document explains the main protein ingredients used in the healthy recipe collection.

Main protein ingredients:
1. GO or Pro protein yogurt:
   - Usually provides about 20-25g protein per container.
   - Used in yogurt bowls, mango bowls, apple pomegranate bowls, and protein desserts.

2. Protein powder:
   - Usually provides about 20-25g protein per scoop, depending on the brand.
   - Used in protein pancakes and protein energy balls.

3. Eggs:
   - Used in the protein pancakes.
   - Two eggs add extra protein and help the pancakes hold together.

4. Cottage cheese:
   - Can be used in bowls to add extra protein and thickness.
   - Works well with strawberries or other fruit.

5. Edamame:
   - Used in the poke bowl.
   - Adds plant-based protein and fiber.

6. Feta cheese:
   - Used in the beet, walnut, and raspberry salad.
   - Adds protein and salty flavor.

7. Salmon, chicken, tofu, or another main protein:
   - Used in the high-protein poke bowl.
   - The protein choice can be changed depending on diet preference.
