Recipe name: Baked Lemon Salmon
Category: High-protein fish dinner
Image description: Salmon fillets baked on a tray with lemon slices and seasoning.

Ingredients:
- 4-6 salmon fillets
- 1 lemon, sliced
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- Pinch of salt
- Optional: honey, soy sauce, chili flakes, parsley, or dill

Steps:
1. Preheat oven to 190°C.
2. Place salmon fillets on a baking tray lined with baking paper.
3. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, black pepper, salt, and optional honey or soy sauce.
4. Brush the mixture over the salmon.
5. Place one lemon slice on each fillet.
6. Bake for 12-18 minutes, depending on salmon thickness.
7. Serve with roasted vegetables, salad, rice, potatoes, or yogurt sauce.

Protein notes:
- Salmon is the main protein source and also provides healthy fats.
- Estimated protein: 28-40g per fillet, depending on size.

Estimated nutrition per fillet:
- Calories: 280-450
- Protein: 28-40g
- Carbohydrates: very low
- Fat: medium to high because salmon contains healthy fats

Diet tags: High protein, fish, dinner, omega-3, meal prep, oven-baked.

Tips:
- Do not overbake salmon; it should stay juicy inside.
- Serve with the sheet-pan roasted vegetables for a balanced meal.
- This recipe is good for meal prep because salmon reheats well with rice or vegetables.
