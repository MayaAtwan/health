Document title: Protein Pancakes with Strawberries and Blueberries

Recipe name: Protein Pancakes with Berries
Category: High-protein breakfast
Source image description: Small golden pancakes served on a white rectangular plate with sliced strawberries and blueberries.
Main protein source: Protein powder and eggs
Estimated protein: About 35-45g per serving, depending on the protein powder scoop
Estimated calories: About 300-420 calories per serving, depending on toppings
Diet tags: High protein, quick breakfast, low ingredient, sweet breakfast

Ingredients:
- 1 scoop vanilla or neutral protein powder
- 2 eggs
- Small pinch of salt
- Optional: 1-2 tablespoons water or milk if the batter is too thick
- Strawberries, sliced
- Blueberries
- Optional: cinnamon, vanilla extract, or sugar-free syrup

Steps:
1. Add the protein powder, eggs, and a small pinch of salt to a bowl.
2. Whisk until the mixture becomes smooth. If it is too thick, add a small amount of water or milk.
3. Heat a non-stick pan on low to medium heat.
4. Pour small circles of batter into the pan.
5. Cook for about 1-2 minutes on the first side, until the pancake holds its shape.
6. Flip carefully and cook the other side for about 30-60 seconds.
7. Serve with sliced strawberries and blueberries.

Healthy notes:
This recipe is very simple and high in protein because it uses a full scoop of protein powder and two eggs. The berries add sweetness, fiber, and color without needing much added sugar.
