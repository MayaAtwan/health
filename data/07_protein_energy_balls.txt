Document title: Protein Energy Balls with Chocolate Drizzle

Recipe name: Protein Energy Balls
Category: Healthy snack or dessert
Source image description: Small round energy balls on a plate, drizzled with melted chocolate.
Main protein source: Protein powder, nut butter, and oats
Estimated protein: About 5-8g per ball, depending on size and protein powder
Estimated calories: About 90-140 calories per ball
Diet tags: High protein snack, meal prep, sweet snack, no bake

Ingredients:
- 1 cup oats
- 1 scoop vanilla or chocolate protein powder
- 1/2 cup peanut butter or almond butter
- 2-3 tablespoons honey or date syrup
- 1-2 tablespoons milk or water if needed
- Optional: chia seeds
- Optional: melted dark chocolate for drizzle

Steps:
1. Add oats and protein powder to a bowl.
2. Add peanut butter and honey.
3. Mix until a thick dough forms.
4. If the mixture is too dry, add a little milk or water.
5. Roll into small balls.
6. Drizzle with melted dark chocolate if desired.
7. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes.

Healthy notes:
These energy balls are useful as a sweet high-protein snack. They are good for meal prep because they can be stored in the fridge for several days.
