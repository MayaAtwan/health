Recipe name: Seafood Tomato Stew with Fries
Category: High-protein seafood meal
Image description: A bowl of seafood in orange tomato sauce, served with French fries and lemon.

Ingredients:
- 300-400g mixed seafood: shrimp, mussels, calamari, and small seafood pieces
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/2 onion, finely chopped
- 1 cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
- 1/2 cup water or light fish stock
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- Pinch of salt
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- Optional: chili flakes, parsley, or coriander
- Side: baked or air-fried potato fries

Steps:
1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add onion and garlic and cook until soft and fragrant.
3. Add tomato sauce, water or stock, paprika, black pepper, salt, and optional chili flakes.
4. Simmer the sauce for 5-7 minutes.
5. Add the mixed seafood and cook for 5-8 minutes, until the shrimp turn pink and the seafood is fully cooked.
6. Squeeze lemon juice on top before serving.
7. Serve with fries on the side. For a healthier version, bake or air-fry the potatoes instead of deep-frying.

Protein notes:
- Seafood is the main protein source.
- Estimated protein: 35-50g per serving, depending on seafood amount.

Estimated nutrition per serving:
- Calories: 450-700, depending on the amount and cooking method of the fries.
- Protein: 35-50g
- Carbohydrates: medium to high because of the potatoes
- Fat: medium

Diet tags: High protein, seafood, dinner, tomato sauce, Mediterranean style.

Tips:
- For a lighter meal, replace fries with roasted potatoes, rice, or salad.
- Do not overcook seafood because shrimp and calamari can become rubbery.
