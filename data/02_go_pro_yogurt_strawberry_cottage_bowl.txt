Document title: GO or Pro Yogurt Strawberry Cottage Bowl

Recipe name: Strawberry Protein Yogurt Bowl
Category: High-protein breakfast or snack
Source image description: A bowl with white cottage cheese or protein yogurt mixed with chopped strawberries.
Main protein source: GO or Pro yogurt, cottage cheese, or high-protein dairy
Estimated protein: About 20-25g if using one GO/Pro yogurt container; more if cottage cheese is added
Estimated calories: About 220-350 calories per bowl
Diet tags: High protein, vegetarian, no cook, breakfast, snack

Ingredients:
- 1 container GO or Pro protein yogurt, about 20-25g protein
- 1/2 cup cottage cheese, optional for extra protein
- Fresh strawberries, chopped
- Optional: chia seeds, oats, cinnamon, or a small drizzle of honey

Steps:
1. Add the GO or Pro yogurt to a bowl.
2. Add cottage cheese if you want a thicker bowl and more protein.
3. Chop strawberries and add them on top.
4. Mix gently or leave layered.
5. Add optional chia seeds or oats for more texture.

Healthy notes:
This is a fast no-cook meal. It is useful when you want something sweet, light, and high in protein. The strawberries add natural sweetness.
