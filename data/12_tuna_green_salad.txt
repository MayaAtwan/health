Recipe name: Tuna Green Salad
Category: High-protein salad
Image description: A fresh green salad with tuna, cherry tomatoes, carrots, red pepper, and leafy greens.

Ingredients:
- 1 can tuna in water or olive oil, drained
- 2 cups mixed greens: lettuce, baby leaves, arugula, or spinach
- 1 tomato or a handful of cherry tomatoes, chopped
- 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 small carrot, sliced into sticks
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- Pinch of salt
- Black pepper
- Optional: cucumber, corn, olives, or boiled egg

Steps:
1. Place the mixed greens in a large bowl.
2. Add chopped tomatoes, red pepper, carrot, and any optional vegetables.
3. Drain the tuna and place it on top of the salad.
4. Mix lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
5. Pour the dressing over the salad and serve immediately.

Protein notes:
- Tuna is the main protein source.
- Estimated protein: 25-35g per bowl, depending on the tuna can size.

Estimated nutrition per serving:
- Calories: 180-320, depending on tuna type and oil amount.
- Protein: 25-35g
- Carbohydrates: low
- Fat: low to medium

Diet tags: High protein, low calorie, salad, quick lunch, tuna, meal prep.

Tips:
- Use tuna in water for a lower-calorie version.
- Add boiled egg or cottage cheese if you want more protein.
- This recipe is good for lunch because it is light but filling.
