Document title: High-Protein Poke Bowl with Edamame and Avocado

Recipe name: High-Protein Poke Bowl
Category: High-protein lunch or dinner
Source image description: White bowls filled with rice or grains, cooked protein cubes, edamame, avocado, carrot, cucumber, radish, cherry tomatoes, sesame seeds, and dark sauce.
Main protein source: Salmon, chicken, tofu, or another cooked protein plus edamame
Estimated protein: About 30-45g per bowl, depending on the protein portion
Estimated calories: About 550-750 calories per bowl
Diet tags: High protein, meal prep, colorful bowl, lunch, dinner

Ingredients:
- 1 cup cooked rice, quinoa, or cauliflower rice
- 120-150g cooked salmon, chicken breast, tofu, or another protein
- 1/2 cup edamame
- 1/2 avocado, sliced
- Carrot sticks
- Cucumber slices
- Radish slices
- Cherry tomatoes
- Sesame seeds
- Teriyaki sauce, soy sauce, or low-sugar sauce
- Optional: green onion or herbs

Steps:
1. Add cooked rice or grains to a bowl.
2. Add cooked salmon, chicken, tofu, or your chosen protein.
3. Add edamame, avocado, carrot, cucumber, radish, and tomatoes around the bowl.
4. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
5. Drizzle with teriyaki sauce or soy sauce.
6. Serve immediately or pack for meal prep.

Healthy notes:
This is a strong high-protein meal because it combines a main protein with edamame. The vegetables add fiber and color, while avocado adds healthy fats.
