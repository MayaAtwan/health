Document title: Beet, Feta, Walnut, and Raspberry Salad

Recipe name: Beet Feta Walnut Raspberry Salad
Category: Healthy salad
Source image description: A colorful salad in a pink bowl with greens, beetroot, cherry tomatoes, feta cubes, black olives, walnuts, and raspberries.
Main protein source: Feta cheese and walnuts
Estimated protein: About 12-18g per large bowl, more if chicken, tuna, eggs, or extra cheese are added
Estimated calories: About 350-500 calories per large bowl
Diet tags: Vegetarian, salad, colorful, Mediterranean style, healthy fats

Ingredients:
- Mixed greens or arugula
- Cooked beetroot, chopped
- Cherry tomatoes, halved
- Feta cheese, cubed
- Black olives, sliced
- Walnuts
- Fresh raspberries
- Olive oil
- Lemon juice or balsamic vinegar
- Salt and black pepper

Steps:
1. Add the mixed greens to a large bowl.
2. Add chopped beetroot and halved cherry tomatoes.
3. Add feta cubes, olives, walnuts, and raspberries.
4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice or balsamic vinegar.
5. Season with salt and black pepper.
6. Toss gently and serve fresh.

Healthy notes:
This salad contains protein from feta and walnuts, healthy fats from olive oil and walnuts, and vitamins from the vegetables and berries. For a higher-protein version, add grilled chicken, boiled eggs, tuna, tofu, or more feta.
