Document title: Mango Protein Yogurt Bowl with Pistachios

Recipe name: Mango Protein Yogurt Bowl
Category: High-protein breakfast or dessert
Source image description: Coconut-style bowls filled with white yogurt, mango cubes, and chopped pistachios.
Main protein source: GO or Pro yogurt
Estimated protein: About 20-25g per bowl if using one GO/Pro yogurt container
Estimated calories: About 300-450 calories per bowl
Diet tags: High protein, tropical, no cook, vegetarian, breakfast, dessert

Ingredients:
- 1 container GO or Pro protein yogurt, about 20-25g protein
- 1/2 to 1 mango, diced
- 1 tablespoon chopped pistachios
- Optional: chia seeds
- Optional: coconut flakes
- Optional: small drizzle of honey

Steps:
1. Add GO or Pro yogurt to a bowl.
2. Dice the mango and place it on top of the yogurt.
3. Add chopped pistachios.
4. Add optional chia seeds or coconut flakes.
5. Serve cold.

Healthy notes:
This bowl is sweet, fresh, and high in protein when made with GO or Pro yogurt. Mango adds natural sweetness, and pistachios add crunch and healthy fats.
