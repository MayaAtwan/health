Document title: Strawberry Protein Cream Pastry

Recipe name: Strawberry Protein Cream Pastry
Category: Protein dessert
Source image description: A rectangular puff pastry dessert filled with white cream and topped with sliced strawberries and powdered sugar.
Main protein source: Protein yogurt, protein cream, or high-protein pudding
Estimated protein: About 15-25g per serving if filled with protein yogurt or protein pudding
Estimated calories: About 300-500 calories per serving, depending on pastry size and filling
Diet tags: Dessert, sweet, protein dessert, strawberry

Ingredients:
- 1 sheet puff pastry
- 1 container GO or Pro vanilla yogurt, about 20-25g protein, or high-protein pudding
- Fresh strawberries, sliced
- Optional: powdered sugar for decoration
- Optional: vanilla extract

Steps:
1. Bake the puff pastry according to the package instructions until golden and crisp.
2. Let the pastry cool completely.
3. Slice the pastry horizontally or use two pastry layers.
4. Spread GO/Pro yogurt or protein pudding between the layers.
5. Add sliced strawberries on top.
6. Add a small amount of powdered sugar if desired.
7. Slice and serve.

Healthy notes:
This is more of a balanced dessert than a low-calorie meal. To make it lighter, use less pastry and more protein yogurt with fresh strawberries.
